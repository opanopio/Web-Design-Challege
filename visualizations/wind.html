Wind Speed

The above scatter plot shows the average wind speed of each city in the dataset vs. latitude.

This plot shows that there is little to no correlation between wind speed and latitude. A quick glance suggests that cities at the equator may tend to have slightly lower levels of wind speed, and that more extreme wind speeds occur further into the northern hemisphere. Despite this observation, we cannot conclude that latitude is a primary factor in a location's average wind speed.
